About Claude

Claude is an AI assistant built by Anthropic, designed from the ground up to be safe, accurate, and genuinely useful. Whether you're working through a complex problem, drafting something creative, or just looking for a thoughtful conversation partner, Claude is built to help — without shortcuts on honesty or safety.

Anthropic is an AI safety company founded with the belief that the most transformative and potentially dangerous technology in human history deserves to be built carefully. The team focuses on alignment research and responsible deployment, so that as AI becomes more powerful, it remains beneficial to everyone.

You can talk to Claude directly at claude.ai, or download the app on desktop and mobile. Follow @claudeai on Instagram to see how Claude is being used by problem solvers around the world every day.
